## üìê CONSTITUTIONAL PRINCIPLES

### Zero-Loss Evidence Doctrine
- Original evidence never modified
- All brains produce findings, not modifications
- Findings are layered, not replacing source data
- Complete evidence + analysis preserved

### Triple-AI Consensus Doctrine
- Every finding verified by 3 independent paths
- Contradictions flagged, not resolved by default
- Evidence remains immutable; interpretation is layered

### Nine-Brain Independence
- Each brain operates independently
- No hierarchy, bias, or overwrite between brains
- All feed to Synthesis Brain for correlation
- No brain may be removed or bypassed
